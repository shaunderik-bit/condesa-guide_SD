Kitchen

Drinking WaterThe tap water is not recommended for drinking. Please use bottled or filtered water for drinking purposes.
Stove & OvenGas-powered. Please make sure all burners are off and knobs are fully closed after use.
Dishwasher
  1. Open the dishwasher door.
  2. Press the power button on the left.
  3. Press P until it reaches 1:30 for larger loads or 40 minutes for smaller loads.
  4. Close the door to begin.
