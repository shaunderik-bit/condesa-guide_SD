House Rules

To help maintain a positive relationship with the building and neighbors, please observe the following:

  • The building key card is for registered guests only and may not be shared with others.
  • Please ensure the main building entrance door closes securely behind you when entering or exiting.
  • Quiet hours begin at 10:00 PM Sunday–Thursday and 11:30 PM Friday–Saturday.
  • Small gatherings and dinner parties (maximum 8 guests) are welcome. Larger parties, events, or celebrations are not permitted. The sublessor is responsible for any damage, excessive cleaning, fines, or building violations resulting from visiting guests.
  • No smoking, vaping, or pets are permitted in the apartment. This includes visiting pets.
  • Please turn off gas burners, faucets, fans, heaters, irons, and other small appliances when not in use.
  • Overnight guests require prior written approval from the host.
  • Please remove shoes while inside the apartment.
  • For security reasons, do not provide building access, keys, or entry instructions to anyone not approved by the host.
  • Please report any damage, maintenance concerns, furniture scratches, tears, appliance issues, or property damage as soon as they occur so they can be addressed promptly.
  • Please return all electronics, remotes, chargers, speakers, and small appliances to their original locations when not in use.
EmergenciesIn the event of an emergency, please contact Shaun immediately and notify Tomas, the building doorman. A fire blanket is located beneath the kitchen sink.
